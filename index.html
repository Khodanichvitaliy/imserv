Our History

IMS, originally International Media Services, was founded back in 1989 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. With a primary focus on print media advertising, IMS soon began its rapid growth in the media and advertising markets within the MENA area, and transformed into a regional Media House. Due to our quick expansion satellite offices were established to better serve our clients’ needs

Our Vision

With the dominance of the digital world in direct correlation with the decline of traditional media, IMS pivoted its strategy to the fintech industry. Today we are a private wealth management and investment company catering to and managing the wealth of our shareholders. Having a long history of industry leadership and innovation, IMS has never stopped looking ahead. As the culture within our organization continues to be, a superb mixture of wisdom and youth leads the company forward today, by developing top-of-the-line trading technologies and data analytics software to complement our institutional trading, and diversifying our investment portfolio in various asset classes

Strategy and Approach 

IMS utilizes its proprietary software development and data analysis techniques to optimize its investments for growth. By incorporating experienced financial software developers with knowledgeable economics experts, IMS’ fintech tools and data solutions in combination with our trading algorithms take on a unique approach to how we tackle the global financial markets. Our propriety software has been in development for over 15 years, and IMS continues to grow and expand its proficiency in understanding how the markets operate, while capturing every opportunity that comes in its path.
